About Us

All inclusive Christian rehabilitation and mental wellness center.

Core Values
Our group is founded on five core values and we work hard to ensure these remain at the heart of everything we do with our clients, our staff and our partners.

Human flourishing
We believe every human was created to thrive and flourish. We will exhaust all therapies and avenues to bring clients to a place of human thriving and abundance..

Enduring
We never give up and will work hard to find a solution. We are driven and never see anyone as a lost cause. We are passionate and always go the extra mile for our clients, colleagues and partners .

Holistic
We treat the whole person; mind, body and soul to achieve a healthy recovery. We recognise that one size doesn’t fit all and there is no one answer to achieve recovery. We are committed to involving and supporting the “whole family” in our work.

Action
We do what we say we are going to do, when we say we are going to do it; We take our responsibilities for excellent services seriously & remain committed to learning and improving. We will push the limits, but respect boundaries. We uphold professional conduct in all we do and maintain discreet care for our clients.

Human Dignity
We work hard to treat people with kindness, privacy, dignity and respect. We look out for others, even when it isn’t our job to do so. We respect and support client’s to make informed choices about their life. We value and respect the contribution that families and friends make to our client’s recovery!

Get Help From our Professional Therapist

Our treatment facility offers round-the-clock assistance from a group of trained medical specialists who are devoted to the client’s rehabilitation. Our clinical team is devoted to giving the support, knowledge, and direction needed to achieve a successful outcome while availing themselves around-the-clock. Our team of doctors, therapists, addiction specialists, and skilled support staff will see our clients’ through their customized rehab program from beginning to end, making sure they have all the skills, resources, and expert insight needed to conquer addiction.

With a foundation built upon the success and principles of our overseas partners we are here to address not only the surface-level challenges but also the underlying factors that contribute to addiction and disorder!
